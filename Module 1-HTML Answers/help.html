How do I register for an event?

  • Go to the Register section from the top navigation bar.
  • Fill in your details and select your desired event type and date.
  • Click Register to submit your registration.
  • A confirmation message will appear below the form.

How can I find events near me?

  • Navigate to the Find Events section.
  • Click the Find Nearby Events button.
  • If prompted, allow the browser to access your location.
  • Your coordinates will be displayed, helping you find local events.

How do I save my event preferences?

  • Go to the Preferences section.
  • Select your favorite event type from the dropdown.
  • Your preference will be saved and remembered next time you visit.
  • To clear your saved preferences, click Clear Preferences.

How do I give feedback?

  • Open the Feedback section.
  • Enter your phone number (10 digits), select an event fee, and type your feedback.
  • Click Submit Feedback to send your feedback.
  • You can double-click the event image to enlarge it.
